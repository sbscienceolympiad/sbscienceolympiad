About Our Club

Welcome to the South Brunswick Science Olympiad Club! Founded in 2008, our club is dedicated to fostering a deep love for science among students through engaging, hands-on challenges that span a diverse array of STEM fields.

Throughout the year, our team comes together to prepare for competitions that challenge our skills in physics, chemistry, biology, engineering, and build events. These competitions give us a chance to apply what we've learned in the classroom to real-world challenges, helping us develop problem-solving skills, teamwork, and a deeper understanding of science.

Whether you're an experienced competitor or just getting started, South Brunswick Science Olympiad offers a great place to learn, grow, and take on new challenges. Join us as we explore, push our limits, and accomplish more together.

What We Do

From competitions to team building, discover what makes our Science Olympiad club special

Compete!

Our club participates in a range of Science Olympiad competitions at the local and state levels, as well as at prestigous universities during invitationals where we work hard to win as a team!

Practice!

Preparation is key to our success, we spend our Fridays preparing for our events with our partners. Whether taking a practice tests or obtaining valuable data, these meetings are a core part of our success!

Have Fun!

Science Olympiad isn't all work; we believe in the value of team chemistry! During our competitions we find the time to eat out, walk around campuses, and enjoy the competition enviroment with friends!
