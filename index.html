South Brunswick Science Olympiad

About Us

Welcome to the South Brunswick Science Olympiad Club! Founded in 2008, our club is dedicated to fostering a deep love for science among students through engaging, hands-on challenges that span a diverse array of STEM fields.

[]

Competitions

Our club participates in a range of Science Olympiad competitions at the local, state levels and prestigous universities during invitationals.

Practice

Preparation is key to our success. We host regular study sessions led by experienced members and guest experts where team members come together to deepen their understanding of the event topics.

Team Bonding

Science Olympiad isn't all work; we believe in the value of team chemistry!
